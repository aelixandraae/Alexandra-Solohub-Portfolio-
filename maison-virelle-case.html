Project Overview

A digital couture gallery, not just a store

Maison Virelle is a conceptual luxury couture e-commerce website created for a high-fashion brand. The project blends minimalist design, editorial aesthetics, and immersive storytelling to transform a traditional online store into a premium digital fashion experience.

The website was envisioned as a digital couture gallery, where each collection is presented as a piece of art. The interface relies on elegant typography, generous white space, and large-scale imagery to create a sophisticated atmosphere.

Problem & Goal

01

Problem

Most fashion e-commerce platforms prioritize catalog efficiency over brand atmosphere — losing the sense of exclusivity that haute couture demands. Users feel like they're browsing a generic store, not entering a world of luxury.

02

Goal

Create an e-commerce experience that feels like stepping into a private couture showroom — where every interaction reinforces exclusivity, every page tells a story, and the path from discovery to purchase feels effortless and refined.

UX Research — User Personas

3 personas · who shops at Maison Virelle

Understanding our users was the foundation of every design decision.

Mia, 22

Young Fashion Enthusiast

Motivations

  • Explore trendy couture collections
  • Create a personal wishlist
  • Follow new arrivals
  • Feel connected to the brand

Obstacles

  • Limited budget for luxury
  • Overwhelmed by too many options
  • Needs clear visuals & easy nav

Alexandra, 32

Fashion Business Woman

Motivations

  • Purchase elegant evening couture
  • Stand out at events
  • Save favorite looks to wishlist
  • Shop quickly & efficiently

Obstacles

  • Websites feel cheap or outdated
  • Difficulty finding premium sizing
  • Complicated checkout process

Sofia, 27

Bride-to-Be

Motivations

  • Find a unique wedding dress
  • Feel luxurious and confident
  • Book a private fitting
  • Discover premium collections

Obstacles

  • Hard to find original designs
  • Fear of poor quality fabrics
  • Limited time before the wedding

User Stories

"As a young fashion lover, I want to browse visually inspiring collections and save favorite looks, so that I can build my dream wardrobe."

— Mia, 22

"As a business woman attending luxury events, I want to discover sophisticated couture with premium presentation, so that my looks reflect my status and individuality."

— Alexandra, 32

"As a bride searching for a couture dress, I want to explore exclusive collections and book a fitting easily, so that I find a gown that feels luxurious on my wedding day."

— Sofia, 27

Design Process

1

Research

Analyzed luxury fashion competitors — Chanel, Valentino, Jacquemus — studied user behavior on premium e-commerce.

2

User Personas

Created 3 detailed personas: Mia (fashion enthusiast), Alexandra (business woman), Sofia (bride-to-be).

3

Wireframe

Low-fidelity wireframes for all 4 key pages — homepage, catalog, product page, checkout flow.

4

Design

High-fidelity UI in Figma — oversized typography, editorial photography, luxury monochromatic palette.

5

Develop

Coded in HTML/CSS/JS — responsive, with smooth interactions, wishlist, cart and product pages.
