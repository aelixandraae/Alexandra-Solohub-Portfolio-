Project Overview

Luxury travel — presented as an editorial experience

Travel Lux Odessa is a premium landing page designed for a travel agency promoting an exclusive 3-day tour across the Odesa region of Ukraine. The primary goal was to present the travel experience in a sophisticated and visually engaging way while guiding visitors toward booking the tour.

The website combines elegant typography, immersive photography, and a clean editorial layout to create a luxurious and memorable user experience — inspired by luxury travel magazines and high-end hospitality brands.

Problem & Goal

01

Problem

Travel agencies often struggle to communicate the premium nature of their tours online. Generic websites with stock photos and cluttered layouts fail to evoke the atmosphere of the destination — leaving potential travelers unconvinced and unbooking.

02

Goal

Create a landing page that feels like opening a luxury travel magazine — showcasing the Odessa region's beauty through immersive photography and editorial design, while strategically guiding visitors toward booking the 3-day tour.

Tour Itinerary — Key Destinations

Day 1

Sanzheika Beach

Odesa Region · Black Sea Coast

Spend the first day on one of the cleanest sandy beaches of the Odesa region — relax by the sea, enjoy the calm coastal atmosphere and a seaside dinner at sunset.

Day 2

Shabo Wine Culture Center

Shabo · Ukraine

Dedicated to Ukrainian wine culture — walk through vineyards and underground cellars, learn about traditional winemaking, and enjoy a guided wine tasting session with a professional sommelier.

Day 3

Bilhorod-Dnistrovskyi Fortress

Bilhorod-Dnistrovskyi · Historic City

Explore one of the largest medieval fortresses in Europe — take a guided tour through the ancient Akkerman Fortress and enjoy panoramic views of the Dniester Estuary.
